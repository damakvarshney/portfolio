## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Should I customize every time I apply?
**A**: Yes! Customization takes 15 minutes and dramatically improves chances of getting past initial screening.

### Q: Can I use the Master Resume to apply?
**A**: Not recommended. It's too long (3-4 pages) and not role-specific. Always customize to 1-2 pages.

### Q: How do I handle job descriptions with contradictory requirements?
**A**: Prioritize technical skills over "nice to haves". Lead with your strongest match.

### Q: What if the JD doesn't match my experience well?
**A**: See Customization Guide "Red Flags to Avoid" section. Sometimes a company isn't right for you—move on.

### Q: How often should I update the Master Resume?
**A**: After every major achievement or quarterly (whichever comes first). Keep it fresh.

### Q: Should I have one resume or multiple versions?
**A**: Have ONE Master (never submit), then create role-specific versions as you target different roles.

### Q: Can I use these templates for LinkedIn?
**A**: Yes! Your LinkedIn summary should echo your resume summary. Use the same headlines and key metrics.
