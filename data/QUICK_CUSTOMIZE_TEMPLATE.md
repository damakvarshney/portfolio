## COMMON PATTERNS

### Emphasize vs. De-emphasize
| If JD is... | Emphasize | De-emphasize |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| AI/ML focus | Vertex AI, RAG, Claude Code, agentic workflows | OTC markets domain, operations initiatives |
| Product/PM focus | User adoption, 1,500+ users, 80%+ adoption, 70% self-service | Technical implementation details, algorithms |
| Strategy/Ops focus | FTE savings, cost impact, time reduction, 4.1 FTE, 90% improvement | Technical depth, algorithms, specific tech stack |
| Fintech domain | OTC markets, LSEG Pricing, KYC/AML, SOX, financial regulations | Generic operations, non-fintech AI products |
| Startup energy | Full-stack, rapid shipping, end-to-end ownership, scrappy | Enterprise bureaucracy, lengthy processes |

## PRO TIPS

1. **Lead with Numbers**: Always open achievement bullets with quantified impact
2. **Use JD Language**: If JD says "RAG", use "RAG" not "retrieval augmented generation"
3. **Action Verbs**: "Architected", "Designed", "Delivered", "Achieved", "Led"
4. **Avoid Fluff**: Every sentence should add information or impact
5. **Test Read**: Read your customized summary out loud. Does it feel authentic?
6. **Keep Master Clean**: Never edit the master resume; always create new versions
7. **Version Control**: Date your customized versions for easy tracking
