# Resume Customization Guide for DAMAK VARSHNEY

## Overview

Your master resume is a **comprehensive repository** of all achievements, skills, and experiences. This guide shows you how to extract and tailor content based on job descriptions you receive.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP CUSTOMIZATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Analyze the Job Description
When you receive a JD, identify:
- **Role Type**: PM, Engineer, Strategy, Analyst, Hybrid
- **Key Skills Required**: Technical stack, domain expertise, soft skills
- **Keywords**: Identify 5-7 critical terms used repeatedly in JD
- **Pain Points**: What problem is this role solving?
- **Company Stage**: Startup, Scaleup, Enterprise

### Step 2: Map Your Experience to JD
- Scan your master resume for matching achievements
- Extract metrics and outcomes that align with JD requirements
- Identify which projects are most relevant
- Note any gaps to address in cover letter

### Step 3: Customize Each Section
See detailed tactics below for each role type.

## CUSTOMIZATION CHECKLIST

### Before Submitting Customized Resume:
- [ ] JD keywords appear in your resume (naturally, not stuffed)
- [ ] Top 3 achievements directly align with JD requirements
- [ ] Metrics are prominent and quantified
- [ ] No irrelevant sections (keep total length 1-2 pages)
- [ ] Summary/headline speaks directly to role
- [ ] Skills section emphasizes JD-relevant skills
- [ ] Spellcheck and formatting review
- [ ] Consistent tense (past for previous roles, present for current)
- [ ] ATS-friendly formatting (no columns, tables, complex formatting)

### Content Length Guide:
- **Achievement sections**: 2-3 bullets each (bullet = 1-2 sentences)
- **Summary**: 3-4 sentences maximum
- **Each role**: 4-6 achievements (tailor count based on relevance)
- **Skills section**: Only include skills relevant to JD
- **Total length**: 1-2 pages

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

❌ **Don't**:
- Remove all fintech/domain content for pure tech roles (it's a differentiator)
- Exaggerate or misrepresent metrics
- Use jargon the role doesn't understand
- Make the resume longer than 1-2 pages
- Remove your portfolio website link
- Forget to proofread before submitting
- Include irrelevant achievements just to fill space

✅ **Do**:
- Lead with your strongest achievements for that role
- Show genuine excitement for the role (via summary/cover letter)
- Keep metrics visible and highlighted
- Tailor without changing facts
- Maintain your unique positioning (rare combo: product + domain expertise)
- Practice talking through your achievements
